Dr. Norman Borlaug

About Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist and humanitarian who led initiatives worldwide that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution. Borlaug was awarded multiple honors for his work, including the Nobel Peace Prize, the Presidential Medal of Freedom, and the Congressional Gold Medal.

Timeline

  • 1914: Born in Cresco, Iowa.
  • 1944: Joined the Rockefeller Foundation's Mexican Agriculture Program.
  • 1960s: Led initiatives that contributed to the Green Revolution.
  • 1970: Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 2009: Passed away in Dallas, Texas.
